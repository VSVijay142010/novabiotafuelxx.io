Introducing you the future gen technology:Biodiesel

Biodiesel is a renewable, clean-burning fuel made from natural sources like vegetable oils, animal fats, and recycled cooking grease. Unlike traditional fossil fuels, biodiesel reduces greenhouse gas emissions, contributing to a cleaner environment. It can be used in diesel engines without modifications and is often blended with petroleum diesel to enhance fuel performance. The production process involves converting oils and fats into biodiesel through a chemical reaction known as transesterification. This results in two products: biodiesel and glycerin, a by-product used in soaps and cosmetics. Biodiesel is biodegradable, non-toxic, and significantly reduces carbon emissions, making it a sustainable alternative to conventional diesel.
